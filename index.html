Spotify

Spotify’s recommendations use how people listen on the platform. We seed it with albums from your Discogs collection, then suggest tracks that fit those seeds. Saving a playlist requires the playlist scope — if “Save as Spotify playlist” fails with a permission error, use Disconnect Spotify and connect again.

This build uses a built-in Spotify app Client ID.

Create an app at developer.spotify.com/dashboard . Under Redirect URIs, add the value below exactly (same https/http, path, and trailing slash). It must match what this site sends to Spotify — copy from here, not from memory.
